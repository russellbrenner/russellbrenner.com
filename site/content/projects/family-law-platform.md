## The problem

Family law proceedings are document-heavy and procedurally complex. Court-compliant documents require specific templates. Evidence needs to be organised and linked to specific issues. Pre-filing validation catches errors before they're rejected.

Most of that work is procedural, not legal. It doesn't require a solicitor's judgment. It requires time, precision, and familiarity with the requirements.

## What it does

Automates the procedural and document-intensive work in family law matters.

**Core capabilities:**

- Document pipeline: draft in plain language, receive court-compliant output in the correct template
- Pre-filing validation: paragraph numbering, cross-reference integrity, draft annotation detection, PII pattern detection, citation verification
- Evidence management: email evidence indexed by issue with relevance scoring
- Filing bundle assembly: annexures organised, highlights applied, page stamps applied

**Workflow templates:** Individual workflows for paralegals and solicitors. No AI jargon. Outputs in court-native language.

## Why it exists

The tooling was built under real conditions during a personal family law matter. That experience is the reason it exists — not as an academic exercise, but as a practical response to navigating a system that is genuinely hard to navigate.
